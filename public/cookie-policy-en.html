Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Killer Sudoku Score uses cookies and local storage technologies to improve your experience.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve website functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies: Remember your language settings, game progress, and user preferences.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage and improve user experience.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your game progress, settings, and statistics so you can continue playing on your next visit.

How to Manage Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics) to analyze website usage, and these services may set their own cookies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
