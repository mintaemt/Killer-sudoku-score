Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Killer Sudoku Score collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service: username (for leaderboard participation), game statistics (scores, completion times), and technical data (browser type, device information).

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to: provide game functionality, maintain leaderboards, improve user experience, and ensure service security. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to remember your preferences, game progress, and language settings. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights. You can also opt out of leaderboard participation by using visitor mode.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through our application or website.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
